Anthony Mackie Set To Host 2021 ESPYS - See The Full List of Nominees Now!

The 2021 ESPYs are almost here – and Anthony Mackie will be hosting!

“I’m excited to be hosting this year’s ESPYS and to celebrate the gift that sports have been this past year,” the Falcon and The Soldier star shared about the hosting gig.

Anthony added, “They have allowed us to cheer on our favorite teams and athletes together at a time when many felt so alone. As an avid sports fan myself, I can’t wait to meet some of my favorite athletes and celebrate their accomplishments!”

ABC and ESPN also announced the full list of nominations today, and voting has already begun at ESPYS.com for super sports fans.

The award show will relive the highlight moments of the year, honor leading athletes and performances, and bring together the sports industry’s fan favorites and biggest stars.

The show will also showcase the achievements of inspiring and courageous athletes who embody the spirit of “The ESPYS” through three pillar awards – the Arthur Ashe Award for Courage, the Jimmy V Award for Perseverance and The Pat Tillman Award for Service.

The 2021 ESPYS will take place on Saturday, July 10, and broadcast live on ABC at 8/7c.
